Chicken

One can eat chicken on a daily basis. However, it is good to use cooking methods like boiling, grilling, roasting or baking rather than frying as it can lead to various health problems. People with kidney problems, chronic heart disease and dyslipidemia should avoid its intake. Chicken has long been considered a healthy alternative to red meat.

Shushi

Sushi is the first name that strikes us when Japanese cuisine is mentioned. Sushi is perhaps the best known Japanese food dish to the outside world. It is a recipe that consists of rice and fish with a flavor of vinegar. However, there are many variations of Sushi that are lesser known by people outside Japan.

Beef

Beef is the culinary name for meat from cattle, particularly skeletal muscle. Humans have been eating beef since prehistoric times. Beef is a source of protein and nutrients. Trimmings, on the other hand, are usually mixed with meat from older, leaner (therefore tougher) cattle, are ground, minced or used in sausages.
